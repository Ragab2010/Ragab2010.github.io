This folder is intended to hold assets for the portfolio site.

Place your resume PDF here as:

  Ragab_resume.pdf

Examples:

  cp /path/to/Ragab_resume.pdf ./assets/Ragab_resume.pdf

After placing the PDF the homepage will preview the resume and the "Download Resume" button will work.

If you want me to add the PDF into the repository, upload it or tell me the exact path and I'll add it for you.
